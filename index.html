About Us

Founded in 2024, OverBed™ emerged from a simple observation: too many professionals were sacrificing their productivity and well-being to the convenience of bed-based work. Our founder, after experiencing chronic back pain from using an overbed table, decided to champion the cause of proper workspace solutions.

Our mission is to help professionals reclaim their dignity and productivity through proper workspace solutions. We believe in the power of dedicated work environments to transform not just how you work, but how you feel about your work. Our core values of professionalism, well-being, and work-life balance drive everything we do.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the health risks of using an overbed table?

Extended use of overbed tables can lead to poor posture, neck strain, back pain, and decreased productivity. Studies show that improper ergonomics can cause long-term health issues.

How long does it take to transition to a proper desk setup?

Most users report feeling more productive within the first week. Full adaptation typically takes 2-3 weeks as you develop new, healthier work habits.

What's the ROI of switching to a proper workspace?

Users report up to 40% increased productivity, better client relationships, and improved work-life balance. The investment typically pays for itself within 3 months.

How do I create an effective workspace in a small area?

We offer consultation services to help optimize even the smallest spaces for maximum productivity. Simple solutions like corner desks and vertical storage can transform any space.

What support do you offer during the transition?

We provide comprehensive guides, ergonomic assessments, and personalized recommendations to ensure your transition is smooth and successful.
