Our Commitment to Transparency

At overbed-tables.com, we believe in complete transparency regarding our business relationships. This disclosure explains how we earn income through affiliate partnerships while maintaining our commitment to providing unbiased, helpful content to our visitors.

Affiliate Partnerships

We participate in affiliate programs with Amazon and ShareASale. This means we may earn commissions when you make purchases through links on our website. These partnerships allow us to:

  • Continue providing free, high-quality content
  • Research and review products thoroughly
  • Maintain and improve our website
  • Support our mission of promoting proper workspace ergonomics

How This Affects You

Our affiliate partnerships do not:

  • Affect the prices you pay for products
  • Influence our product recommendations or reviews
  • Compromise our editorial integrity
  • Share your personal information with third parties

When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, you're supporting our work at no additional cost to you. We only recommend products we believe will genuinely benefit our readers.

Identification of Affiliate Links

While we strive to identify affiliate links throughout our content, you should assume that any links to products on Amazon or other e-commerce platforms may be affiliate links. We encourage you to do your own research and make purchasing decisions based on your needs and preferences.

Questions?
